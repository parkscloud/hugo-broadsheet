The Downtown Food Festival returns this Saturday and Sunday, bringing together the best of the local culinary scene.

Now in its 15th year, the festival has grown from a small neighborhood gathering to a major regional event attracting thousands of visitors.

## What to Expect

This year's festival features:

- 50 local restaurants and food vendors
- Live cooking demonstrations
- A children's activity area
- Live music on two stages

## Hours and Location

**Saturday:** 11 AM - 9 PM
**Sunday:** 11 AM - 6 PM

The festival takes place in Central Park and along Main Street. Admission is free; food and drinks are pay-as-you-go.

## Getting There

Organizers encourage attendees to use public transportation, as parking will be limited. The downtown transit station is a 5-minute walk from the festival entrance.
