DataFlow, a local technology startup specializing in real-time data analytics, announced today that it has secured $10 million in Series A funding.

The round was led by Venture Capital Partners, with participation from several angel investors.

## What They Do

DataFlow's platform helps businesses make sense of large volumes of streaming data. Their technology is used by companies in finance, healthcare, and logistics to make faster, data-driven decisions.

"We're processing billions of events per day for our customers," said CEO Sarah Kim. "This funding will help us scale our infrastructure and expand our team."

## Growth Plans

The company plans to:

1. Double its engineering team from 15 to 30
2. Open a second office on the West Coast
3. Launch new features for enterprise customers

DataFlow was founded in 2023 and has grown to serve over 100 business customers.
