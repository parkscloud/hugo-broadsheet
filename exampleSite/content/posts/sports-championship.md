City FC is heading to the championship final after a thrilling 2-1 semifinal victory last night.

Midfielder Carlos Mendez scored the winning goal in the 89th minute, sending the home crowd into a frenzy.

## Match Recap

The visitors took an early lead in the 23rd minute, silencing the sold-out stadium. City FC equalized just before halftime through a penalty kick converted by striker David Okonkwo.
